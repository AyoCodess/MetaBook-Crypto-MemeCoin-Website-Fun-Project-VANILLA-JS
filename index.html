  • How to buy (step by step)
    Make sure you have your funds converted to Solana ($sol) on a centralized exchange

    1. Create a Solana wallet (we recommend phantom.app) and send the SOL to your Phantom wallet address.

    2. For easy swapping, we recommend using Orca, Raydium or Step.

    3. On any of the above sites, click the button in the top right to ‘connect wallet’.

    4. Now you can easily swap your Solana tokens for $Metabook. Please remember to leave a small amount of SOL for transaction fees. (around 0.002 SOL should be sufficient)

    5. Congratulations, you are now part of our Metabook family!
